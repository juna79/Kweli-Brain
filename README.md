# Kweli Brain

**The Institutional Intelligence Platform for Kweli**

---

## Vision

Kweli Brain is the central operating system behind Kweli.

It is where every conversation, meeting, opportunity, insight, objection and lesson becomes part of the company's institutional knowledge.

It is **not** a CRM.

It is **not** an email automation platform.

It is **not** an AI sales bot.

It is a founder-led intelligence platform designed to help Kweli identify the right opportunities, prepare for meetings, manage relationships, and continuously improve its understanding of every industry it serves.

The goal is simple:

> **Capture knowledge. Improve decisions. Build trust.**

---

# Purpose

Every day, founders spend hours researching companies, preparing for meetings, following up with prospects, updating notes and trying to remember conversations from weeks or months ago.

Kweli Brain exists to eliminate that friction.

It becomes the single source of truth for every business development activity.

Instead of replacing the founder, it amplifies the founder.

---

# Core Principles

## Founder-led

Kweli Brain prepares.

The founder decides.

There is intentionally no automated outreach or autonomous sales.

Every customer relationship remains human.

---

## Institutional Memory

Every meeting, objection, lesson learned and customer insight is stored.

Knowledge compounds instead of disappearing into notebooks, email threads or chat histories.

The platform should become smarter every week because the company becomes smarter every week.

---

## Precision Over Hype

Kweli verifies the authenticity of documents.

Kweli does **not** verify whether the contents of those documents are true.

This distinction is fundamental.

Every recommendation, prompt and sales message must preserve this precision.

---

## Quality Over Quantity

The objective is not to contact the most companies.

The objective is to identify the right organisations where Kweli creates measurable value.

If Kweli is not a good fit, the platform should say so.

# Features

## Executive Dashboard

A real-time overview of:

* Today's priorities
* Upcoming follow-ups
* Overdue follow-ups
* High-priority opportunities
* Opportunities by stage
* Recent activity
* Suggested next actions

---

## Opportunity Management

Track every organisation from first discovery through to pilot and beyond.

Each opportunity contains:

* Company profile
* Industry
* Contacts
* Current stage
* Follow-up reminders
* Meeting history
* Notes
* Objections
* Proposed pilot
* AI-generated prompts

---

## Kweli Brain

The company's institutional knowledge.

Including:

* Product positioning
* What Kweli is
* What Kweli is not
* Industry playbooks
* Objection handling
* Pilot framework
* Pricing
* Discovery methodology
* Sales messaging
* Lessons learned
* Competitive positioning

Everything generated by the platform should reference this knowledge first.

---

## Meeting Intelligence

Prepare for every conversation by generating structured prompts that help:

* Research organisations
* Identify likely champions
* Prepare discovery questions
* Anticipate objections
* Recommend pilots
* Draft follow-up emails
* Summarise meeting notes
* Update the Kweli Brain

---

## Follow-up Manager

Never lose track of a relationship.

Track:

* Last interaction
* Next action
* Follow-up dates
* Opportunity stage
* Meeting outcomes

---

## AI Prompt Generator

Version 1 intentionally contains no AI integration.

Instead, Kweli Brain generates structured prompts that can be copied directly into Claude.

This keeps operating costs at zero while still leveraging advanced reasoning.
